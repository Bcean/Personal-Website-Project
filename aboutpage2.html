About Artist

Birthplace/Hometown
Although I was born in Cameroon, I've spent over 10 years of my life in Canada and more specifically Edmonton Alberta. Part of the benefits of living in Canada for a long time is adapting to cold weather conditions and being forced to find a sense of joy within the harsh snow. Even though, I am Canadian and I have citizenship to accompany that, my Cameroonian ancestry is an integral part of me and I have had the pleasure of visiting where I was born, and having a chance to understand my own culture.
Hobbies
I find a lot of enjoyment in artistic expression like music. Besides being an avid user of Spotify I have over 5 years of trumpet playing experience and have spent most of my junior high and high school years within instrumental band classes as my main extracurricular. To elaborate, most of my senior year of high school was spent in a band room as I dedicated myself to being a part of over 4 school ensembles and was a participating player in my school's brass ensemble which went out of their way to participate in an adjudicated performance.
MST
Most of my time at MST is spent studying but beyond that boring endeavor, I go out of my way to actively participate in different clubs that interest me such as the ACM Security Club, ACM Web, and the International Students Club. Part of my second semester as a freshman is going to become more interesting as I plan to become a part of the S&T Jazz ensemble. Beyond this, I still have more projects an aspirations I plan to fulfill as I become an extensive active member of my school community.
